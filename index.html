A little bit about me

Hello there! My name's Filip and I'm a young man filled with enthusiasm and ambition, working to fulfill my dreams.

​

On this website, I am going to introduce myself and talk about the place where I grew up in, my school life, and things that I like, such as hobbies and travelling!

History

This is the Ribichka, the Macedonian name for the Fish Square, one of the most famous landmarks in the town. It's called that because there's a fountain that's shaped like a fish from bird's view.

My history

I grew up with two elder sisters in a small town in southeastern Macedonia called Radovish. This is also the place where I finished primary and  high school.

​

However, besides my nuclear family, most of my relatives live in next-door city Strumica, who we visit pretty often.

My school

Spending more time in school, I started to love studying. I find it very satisfying when I discover new branches of knowledge and expand my horizons. 

​

Some of my favorite subjects are Maths, Geography, Chemistry and of course, Informatics. 

​

I am now going to college, majoring Software Engineering, in the faculty of FINKI, Skopje. There are also a lot of interesting courses, like Intro to Computer Science, Architecture of Computers, and of course Basics od Web Design.

Hobbies

Programming
